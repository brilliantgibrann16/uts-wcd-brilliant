About the Universe

The story behind my playlists, my productivity habits, and the genres that keep my days moving.

Brilliant

Why music is important to me

For me, music is a personal operating system. It helps me decide the pace of a day, the mood of a study session, and the emotional color of a memory. A good song can make a normal walk feel cinematic, while a familiar playlist can bring back the exact feeling of a specific semester, deadline, or late-night conversation.

I also listen because music makes ordinary routines feel intentional. When I press play, I am not only filling silence; I am choosing an atmosphere. That is why Brilliant's Music Universe is built as more than a list of songs. It is a designed archive of what I hear, feel, and remember.

How playlists help productivity

Playlists help me separate tasks into different mental rooms. When I need to code, I choose cleaner beats and songs that do not interrupt my focus. When I need ideas, I use warmer indie-pop tracks that make the screen feel less heavy. When I need rest, I move into soft R&B and rainy-day songs.

The most useful playlist is the one that reduces decision fatigue. Instead of searching for songs every few minutes, I can stay inside one sound world and keep working. I like to match the playlist to the task, because the right mood makes studying feel smoother and more consistent.

Favorite genres

My favorite genres are indie pop, bedroom pop, Indonesian indie, R&B, dream pop, and alternative rock. I like music that feels emotional without being too dramatic, polished without losing personality, and modern without sounding empty. The sweet spot is melodic, honest, and replayable.

Indie Pop

Bright hooks, gentle guitars, and songs that feel personal without being too quiet.

Bedroom Pop

Soft vocals, intimate production, and late-night lyrics that work well for reflection.

R&B

Smooth rhythm, warm bass, and vocals that make study breaks feel slower.

Alternative Rock

Textured guitars and focused energy for movement, confidence, and long walks.
